When Engineering Consulting Is Needed

When it comes to post-frame construction, many building departments mandate engineering reports. It's essential to work with an engineer who is well-versed in the specific characteristics of these structures.

✓ An inspector questions footing depth ✓ A retrofit condition needs evaluation ✓ A connection detail needs clarification ✓ Wood members have checks or cracks ✓ A truss was field-modified ✓ A post appears misaligned ✓ A hole was drilled through a structural member ✓ A structural component was altered ✓ A homeowner requests structural verification

We Understand How Post Frame Buildings Behave

Post-frame buildings differ from traditional construction methods. They utilize diaphragm action, embedded columns, and distributed load paths. A surface crack doesn't necessarily indicate failure, nor does a drilled hole automatically warrant replacement.
