Structural Engineering for Timber Frame Construction

Timber frame buildings rely on heavy structural members and specialized connections to transfer loads safely. Proper engineering ensures these systems perform correctly and meet building code requirements.

Woodrock Engineering provides structural design, analysis, and stamped drawings for timber frame buildings, supporting both residential and commercial applications.
