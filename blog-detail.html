Understanding Load Paths

The specificity of structural components is determined by various factors, including material properties, geometry, and load conditions.

  • Material properties (e.g. steel, concrete, timber)
  • Geometric properties (e.g. cross-sectional area, moment of inertia)
  • Load conditions (e.g. dead load, live load, wind load)
  • Support conditions (e.g. fixed, pinned, roller)
