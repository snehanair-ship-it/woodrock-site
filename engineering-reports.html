Why Engineering Reports are Required

Many building departments require engineering reports for post-frame construction. You need an engineer who understands the nuances of these unique structures.

Engineering reports are typically requested when:

  • A footing inspection requires documentation
  • Concrete placement needs structural confirmation
  • Embedded posts must be verified
  • Slab thickness or reinforcement is questioned
  • Connections require engineering validation
  • Construction differs from the original drawings
  • An inspector needs formal engineering confirmation
