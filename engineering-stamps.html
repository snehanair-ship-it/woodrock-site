What we do

Builders often feel frustrated with engineering firms that overdesign simple buildings and complicate the process. We focus on practical design and review, aiming to validate and protect your system.

Who This Service Is For

Our Engineering Stamp service is perfect for post frame builders, pole barn contractors, agricultural building companies, barndominium builders, and commercial post frame contractors. If your permit office mandates a licensed engineer's seal, we offer a streamlined and effective solution.

Important Clarification

Engineering Stamp services involve professional structural review and validation. We do not:

  • Provide fabrication shop drawings
  • Assume means-and-methods responsibility
  • Seal drawings without proper review
  • Bypass structural code compliance
