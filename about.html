About Woodrock Engineering

We are a structural engineering firm built around specialized construction systems with a primary focus on post frame construction.

Our Story

Woodrock Engineering was founded with a clear mission: provide practical, code-compliant structural engineering specifically for post frame and related construction systems. We understand that structural engineering should support construction — not complicate it.

With experience across 30+ states and over 1,000 projects engineered nationwide, we bring deep expertise in post frame, timber frame, and outdoor structure engineering.
