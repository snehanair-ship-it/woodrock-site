About Project

Woodrock Engineering provided complete structural engineering services for this custom post frame workshop in Texas. The project required a durable, open-span structure capable of supporting workshop equipment while maintaining long-term structural performance.

Our team delivered stamped engineering drawings and structural calculations to ensure the building met all local code requirements and could move efficiently through the permitting process.

The Challenge

The client required a large, open workshop space without interior columns that could interfere with movement and equipment layout. The structure needed to safely support roof loads, resist wind forces specific to the project location in Texas, and meet all local building code requirements.

In addition, the project required stamped engineering drawings to secure permits and keep the construction timeline on schedule.

The Solution

Woodrock Engineering provided complete structural analysis and stamped engineering drawings tailored to the project's specific requirements. Our team designed a post frame structural system capable of supporting the required loads while maintaining a clear-span interior layout.

We delivered permit-ready engineering documentation that met local code requirements and helped streamline the approval process.
